Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment gateway is best for my business in India?

For most Indian businesses, Razorpay offers excellent support for UPI, cards, and net banking with competitive rates. For international transactions, Stripe or PayPal are recommended. We analyze your specific needs to recommend the best option.

How long does payment gateway integration take?

Simple integration typically takes 3-5 business days. Complex integrations with custom features may take 1-2 weeks. The timeline also depends on how quickly you can provide required business documents for gateway approval.

What documents are required for payment gateway setup?

Typically you need: Business registration documents, PAN card, GST certificate (if applicable), bank account details, cancelled cheque, and identity/address proof of directors. Requirements vary by gateway provider.

Do you provide support after integration?

Yes, all our packages include post-integration support. Basic plan includes 30 days support, Advanced includes 90 days, and Enterprise includes 6 months of support and maintenance. We also offer annual maintenance contracts.

Can I switch payment gateways later if needed?

Absolutely. We design integrations to be modular, making it easier to switch or add additional gateways in the future. We can help you migrate to a different gateway with minimal downtime.
